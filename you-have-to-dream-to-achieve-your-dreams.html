You have to dream to achieve your dreams

Narender Singh February 26, 2020

Everyone sees dreams, fantasies take birth in everyone's mind; But there are very few such people, who try to make them come true. And even less than that there are those people who can really make their dreams come true.

Talking about dreams, is the object of dreams only? No. If there were no dreams, those who saw them would not have considered them related to real life, then we would not have been at the present peak of civilization today. It is due to these dreams that we are able to bring news of space today, are able to visit Moon, have the power to measure the world universe, etc.

Who does not know that the imaginative artist is only able to imagine the artwork in a simple piece of stone. Do not know how many people would have seen birds flying in the sky with a free spirit and thought that they too can roam like birds in the sky. But the Wright Brothers resolved to make their dream come true and they gave us airplanes. James Watt and Edison dreamed and while trying to make them come true, they gave us various tools of science.

Everything we see in the name of civilization and progress in this world is the sum of the dreams of all dreamers. Remove the accounts of dreamers from the history of the world, then see what remains to be read in history?

The dreamer is virtually the harbinger of equality. He is a seeker of realizing his dreams, doing hard labor and continuous practice, uniting his blood and sweat. It is he who has cleared the cut-off forests and has built clean roads for humanity by cutting off the paved paths. Like these seekers of human welfare, do you also wish to dream a dream of freeing humans from fear and bondage. Traditionally, what is available to man is most important in the frequency of imagination ie the power of dreaming.

According to 'Jin Khoja Tin Paiyan', who gather from their life to achieve their goal, they are grateful for their efforts. Fearful of realizing the dream of victory, Japanese youths filled the gap with their corpses and made way for Japanese troops. In 1904, it was the secret of the sensational conquest of a small country, Japan, on a large country like Russia.

Their dreams come true, who feel pleasure in working in the midst of dusty soil, mud-waste, thorn pebbles.
